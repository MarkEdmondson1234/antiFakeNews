								<span class="cb-itemprop" itemprop="reviewBody"><h4>The large-scale leak of confidential data related to French presidential candidate Emmanuel Macron continues to make headlines today, with some claiming to have found dubious information in the 9 gigabytes of files.</h4>
<p>While the veracity of the leaks was unclear yesterday, it has apparently been confirmed by multiple outlets,<a href="http://www.reuters.com/article/us-france-election-cyber-idUSKBN1820QO">including Reuters,</a> which describes the drop as containing &#8220;emails and other campaign documents that Macron&#8217;s campaign said on Friday had been stolen.&#8221;</p>
<p><a href="http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-39830379">According to the BBC,</a> the French media and public have been warned that spreading the data in advance of tomorrow&#8217;s election &#8220;would breach strict election rules and could bring criminal charges.&#8221;</p>
<p>But while the French are keeping relatively mum on the leaks, social media users from the United States are speaking about them loud and clear, sharing some information that  if true  could prove rather embarrassing for Macron.</p>
<p>For example, <a href="http://gotnews.com/breaking-macronleaks-show-emmanuelmacron-gay-mailing-list-top-aides-buying-drugs-online/">Got News</a> claims to have found among the leaked files evidence that Emmanuel Macron is on the mailing list of &#8216;VestiareGay.&#8217;</p>
<p>&#8220;Mailing lists like this are opt-in only,&#8221; the outlet claims. &#8220;This image was found in an e-mail addressed to Emmanuel Macron.&#8221;</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12869" src="https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-gay-1.png" alt="" width="650" height="743" srcset="https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-gay-1.png 650w, https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-gay-1-262x300.png 262w" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" /></p>
<p>The outlet also declares, &#8220;the rumors circulated about French presidential candidate Emmanuel Macron&#8217;s inner circle are true,&#8221; in reference to apparently yet-unfounded<a href="https://www.wesearchr.com/bounties/prove-emmanuel-macron-is-gay-apportez-la-preuve-de-lhomosexu">allegations</a> that Macron is gay.</p>
<h4>Got News also claims to have found evidence that Macron&#8217;s senior campaign member Alain Tourret once ordered a &#8220;designer drug&#8221; using bitcoin.</h4>
<p>Presenting the screenshot below, the outlet alleges that Tourret purchased MMC-3, &#8220;a combination of effects similar to ecstasy and crystal meth.&#8221;</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12871" src="https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-Gay-2.jpg" alt="" width="735" height="697" srcset="https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-Gay-2.jpg 735w, https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-Gay-2-300x284.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 735px) 100vw, 735px" /></p>
<p>&#8220;He had his package sent to a government address, which may not be as reckless as it first appears,&#8221; states the outlet in an apparent reference to the address that has been blurred in the document screenshot above.</p>
<p>&#8220;If this is sent to his public address, as opposed to a home address not known to the public, then, if caught, he can claim to be victims of a prank.&#8221;</p>
<p>The outlet goes on to cite this screenshot, which was allegedly &#8220;retrieved by researchers who are experts in illicit online transactions such as this drug purchase.&#8221;</p>
<p>The screenshot allegedly confirms that the transaction detailed in the previous screenshot took place.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12873" src="https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-Gay-3-740x589.jpg" alt="" width="740" height="589" srcset="https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-Gay-3-740x589.jpg 740w, https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-Gay-3-740x589-300x239.jpg 300w, https://cdn.milo.yiannopoulos.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Macron-Gay-3-740x589-378x300.jpg 378w" sizes="(max-width: 740px) 100vw, 740px" /></p>
